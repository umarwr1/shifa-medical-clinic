About Us

Shifa Medical Clinic is committed to providing the highest quality of patient care. The clinic features trained staff that will optimize the care of each patient. At Shifa Medical Clinic we focus on diagnosing and treating patients of all ages, while emphasizing preventative medicine and the overall health and wellness of its patients.

We understand that there are many factors that can affect health, including exercise, diet, environment and inheritance. At Shifa Medical Clinic, we perform necessary procedures after both risks and benefits have been explained and understood, and written consent has been obtained from the patient.

We provide Family Practice and Walk-In medical services. We are accepting new patients.

Our Mission

To provide family health care to everyone in a timely manner.

Our goal is to build a family medical home to cater to the needs of our patients and their families where they can get their medical needs fulfilled in a comfortable and friendly environment.

We ensure a primary care facility by a team of healthcare professionals. At Shifa Medical Clinic, you can receive our focused attention. We take time to listen to you and help to solve your health concerns.

We create a culture in which you will feel healthy, safe, and valued.

Why Choose Us

"Patients First" is the guiding principle of Shifa Medical Clinic. We strive to provide outstanding and compassionate care and service, every step along the way. Here are some reasons why you choose Shifa Medical Clinic.

  • We believe high-quality medical care should be convenient, affordable, and efficient.
  • Professional care and convenient service.
  • Looking for a better approach to healthcare.
  • We provide Family Practice and Walk-In medical services. We are accepting new patients.

Our Team is Our Strength

We have a supportive, respectful mindset. It is essential to create a positive environment for you. We keep a strong focus on your issues to ensure the best health outcomes. Our team will provide on-time appointments so no more waiting time.
